College Campus
Est. 2007
The college campus has well-furnished facilities and all the modern amenities including Play grounds, indoor games, career counseling / student welfare cell. The campus offers a blend of healthy environment, resources, support and guidance to enhance the quality of student's experience.

library
About. 5000+ books
Our college library contains a diverse collection of over 5000+ books and a spacious reading area. Our librarians are available to assist with research and provide personalized recommendations. Come study and discover new worlds in the quiet comfort of our library.

Computer Laboratories
About. 5000+ PC's
Our college has state-of-the-art computer labs equipped with the latest technology. With a variety of computers, printers, and other resources. Our labs are open extended hours for students and our IT support team is always available for assistance.

Science laboratory
About. 5000+ Sets
The college has well equipped laboratories for imparting practical knowledge in Physics, Chemistry, Zoology and Botany. The aim is to provide students a conceptual as well as practical understanding of the subject through hands on training.

Auditorium
About. 400+ Seats
Our college auditorium is a modern and spacious venue for events and performances. It features state-of-the-art sound, lighting systems, comfortable seating, and a large stage. The auditorium is a versatile space that can accommodate a wide range of events, from lectures and presentations to concerts and theater productions.

Sports
8 sq. km
On the sports front, the College has Cricket Field, Volleyball, Badminton, Basketball Courts, Rooms for Table Tennis and various other indoor games to facilitate all round development following the Indian tradition and culture that empowers every student with confidence to achieve his or her goal in the most conducive environment of the College.

lecture rooms
3 sq km
Our college lecture rooms are modern and well-equipped to support student learning. Each room features comfortable seating and ample space for students to take notes. The lecture rooms are designed for an optimal learning experience, with good lighting, air, and ample room to move around.
